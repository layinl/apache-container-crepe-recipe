Crêpe Recipe

Prep time: 5-10 min

Ingredients:

  • 1 teacup of water
  • 1 egg
  • a pinch of salt
  • 125% teacup of milk
  • 10 drops of vanilla essence
  1. Mix all the ingredients in the blender for 30 seconds
  2. Pour a scoop of the mass in the oil greased frying pan
  3. While cooking, put the filling you like and flid it in half
  4. Fold it again and serve
